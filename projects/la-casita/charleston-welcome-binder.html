🌳 Must-See Attractions

Natural Wonder · Free
Angel Oak Tree
One of the oldest living things east of the Mississippi. Estimated 300-400 years old, standing 65 feet tall with branches spanning 187 feet. Free admission. An absolute must-see while you're here.

🏖 Beaches

Beach · 20 Min Drive
Folly Beach
Charleston's beach town. Six miles of sand, great waves, beach bars, and a laid-back surfer vibe. From La Casita, you're closer than most visitors — about 20 minutes by car.
🅿️ Parking Tips: Arrive before 10am for easy parking. Metered street parking available. For guaranteed spots, drive to Folly Beach County Park at the south end ($15/car). Walk past the gate toward Morris Island Lighthouse for stunning views.

🏙 Downtown Charleston (15 min drive)

Historic downtown is just across the Ashley River. Here are the highlights:

  • 🛍 King Street — Charleston's main shopping and dining strip. Boutiques, galleries, and restaurants for miles.
  • 🌅 The Battery & White Point Garden — Waterfront promenade lined with antebellum mansions. Stunning at sunset.
  • 🏠 Rainbow Row — Iconic pastel-colored houses on East Bay Street. The most photographed spot in Charleston.
  • 🧺 City Market — Outdoor market with local crafts, sweetgrass baskets, and unique souvenirs.
  • ⛲ Waterfront Park — The famous Pineapple Fountain, harbor views, and a perfect sunset walk.
  • 🐢 South Carolina Aquarium — Great for families, right on the harbor.
  • 🏰 Fort Sumter — Where the Civil War began. Take the ferry from Liberty Square.
