## What Are AI Agent Tokens?

AI agent tokens are cryptocurrencies that power autonomous AI systems. These agents can:

- **Trade markets 24/7** without human intervention
- **Manage investment portfolios** across multiple chains
- **Complete tasks for pay** (research, coding, content creation)
- **Interact with other agents** in decentralized networks

The token typically serves as:
1. Payment for the agent's services
2. Governance rights over the agent's decisions
3. A share in the agent's revenue

Think of it like buying equity in a robot that works around the clock.

## Red Flags: How to Spot AI Agent Scams

**98.6% of tokens on pump.fun show rug-pull characteristics.** AI agent tokens are no different. Watch for:

### 1. No Verifiable AI
If you can't see the agent's code, GitHub, or work output — it's probably just a meme coin with "AI" in the name.

### 2. Anonymous Team + No Vesting
Legitimate projects lock founder tokens for 6-12 months. If the team can dump day one, they will.

### 3. No Revenue Model
"AI agent" isn't a business model. Ask: How does this agent make money? Who pays for its services?

### 4. Hype > Substance
If the project is all Twitter threads and no product, run.

### 5. Unrealistic Promises
"100x guaranteed" and "AI that can't lose" are scam signals. Real agents fail sometimes. Honest teams admit it.

## How to Evaluate an AI Agent Token

Before investing, ask:

**1. Can I see the agent working?**
Look for public GitHub repos, live dashboards, or transaction history showing real activity.

**2. What's the token utility?**
Does holding the token give you governance, revenue share, or access? Or is it just speculative?

**3. How is revenue distributed?**
Best projects have on-chain, automated buybacks or distributions. "Trust us" isn't good enough.

**4. What's the vesting schedule?**
Team tokens should be locked 6-12 months minimum. Check the contract.

**5. Who's building it?**
Doxxed teams with track records > anonymous founders every time.
