## Best Practices
- Keep threads 5-10 tweets
- Start with a strong hook (problem or result)
- Use bullet points and line breaks
- Include social proof (testimonials, metrics)
- End with a clear CTA
- Reply to your own thread with extra context or a demo video

## Timing
- Best days: Tue-Thu
- Best times: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM EST
- Avoid: Late Friday, weekends

## Engagement Tactics
- Ask a question in the last tweet
- Reply to early engagers within 5 minutes
- Quote tweet with extra details 2 hours later
- Pin the thread to your profile for 24 hours
