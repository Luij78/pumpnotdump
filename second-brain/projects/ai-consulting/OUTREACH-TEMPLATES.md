Let me know if you're curious.

— Luis

## Notes for Using These Templates

### Personalization Matters
- Always use their real name
- Reference something specific (a listing, mutual connection, their market)
- Don't copy-paste word-for-word—make it sound like you

### Best Practices
- **Follow up 2-3 times** (most deals happen on follow-up)
- **Keep it short** (especially texts/DMs)
- **No hard selling** — offer value, let them come to you
- **Use the beta pitch first** to get testimonials

### Who to Contact First
1. Agents you've worked with before (co-listings, referrals)
2. Agents in your brokerage
3. Agents you've met at events/trainings
4. Agents you admire on social media
5. Cold outreach to local top producers

### Tracking
Keep a simple spreadsheet:

|------|--------------|----------|-----------|--------|

| John Smith | 1/28 | No reply | 2/1 | Follow up |
